Refund Policy

Effective Date: 18 April 2026

This policy outlines the terms for cancellations and refunds for CattleSystem services.

1. Subscription Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellation will stop future billing
  • You will continue to have access until the end of your billing cycle

2. No Refund Policy

All payments made are non-refundable, except in the cases mentioned below.

3. Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds may be considered only under the following conditions:

  • Duplicate payment made by mistake
  • Payment processed but service not activated
  • Technical error caused by our system

4. Non-Eligible Refund Cases

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Lack of usage of the service
  • Dissatisfaction due to expected outcomes (e.g., milk prediction, alerts)
  • User errors or incorrect data input

5. Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

  • Email us at care@cattlesystem.com
  • Include your registered phone number and payment details

We will review your request within 5–7 working days.

6. Refund Processing

If approved, refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method.

7. Pricing Changes

We reserve the right to change pricing at any time. Existing users will not be affected during their active billing cycle.
