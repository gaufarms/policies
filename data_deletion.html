CattleSystem respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data.

1. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data stored with us.

2. What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Personal information (name, phone number)
  • Cattle records (milk, breeding, health data)
  • Usage data and account history

3. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion using the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

  • Email: care@cattlesystem.com
  • Include your registered phone number

Option 2: In-App Request (if available)

You may also request deletion through app settings.

4. Verification

We may verify your identity before processing your request to prevent unauthorized deletion.

5. Processing Time

  • Requests are processed within 7–15 working days
  • You will receive confirmation once completed

6. What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • You will lose access to all data and services
  • This action cannot be reversed

7. Exceptions

We may retain limited data if required for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Dispute resolution

8. Third-Party Data

We may request deletion from third-party service providers, but cannot guarantee timelines controlled by them.
